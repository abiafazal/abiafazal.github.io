Arts and Culture Department

The Riverside High School Arts and Culture Department is a vibrant and dynamic department within Riverside High School, dedicated to fostering creativity, artistic expression, and cultural appreciation among its students. As an integral part of the school's curriculum, the department offers a wide range of artistic disciplines and cultural experiences, providing students with opportunities to explore their talents, develop their skills, and deepen their understanding of various art forms.

Programs and Offerings

Courses Offerings
Visual Arts Drawing, Painting, Sculpture, Ceramics, Photography, Graphic Design
Performing Arts Theater, Dance, Music
Media and Film Studies Media Studies, Filmmaking
Cultural Studies Exploration of different cultures, traditions, and artistic expressions

Extracurricular Activities

  • Art Club
  • Drama Club
  • Dance Ensemble
  • Music Appreciation Society
